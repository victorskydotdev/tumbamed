Our Services

We offer a wide range of medical services to cater for your health needs

Procedure

Punch biopsies, Skin cancer excision, Implanon insertion/removal, Iron infusion, Venesection, Lipoma excisions, Pap smears.

Mental Health

Excellent care

Our team are well trained to give you the best medical attention and help that will facilitate quick recovery. At TMP we care about your health and well-being

Men & Womens Health

Men’s Health: Healthy heart check, General Health check, Women’s Health: Cervical Screening, Contraception, Postnatal care, Menopause.

Preventive Care

Cervical Cancer screening, Osteoporosis screening, Smoking cessation, Alcohol cessation.

Chronic Disease

We have teamwork approach in managing chronic health conditions like: Diabetes mellitus, Hypertension, Heart disease, Asthma, COPD, Osteoarthritis. We organise care plans and have regular follow up with our patients. We have prompt recall system.

Vacination

Childhood immunizations, Seasonal Flu vaccinations, Travel Vaccinations, Pre-employment immunity check.

Health Check

Four-year-old kid health check, Healthy heart check, Diabetic risk assessment, 45 year to 49 year old health check, 75 year old health assessment, Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander health assessment.

Other Services

Workers compensation, Employment medicals, Commercial drivers licence assessment, Age retest for drivers licence, ECG, INR testing.
