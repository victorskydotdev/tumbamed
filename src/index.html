What We Offer
Comprehensive Medical
Services

We provide a wide range of healthcare services for individuals and families across Tumbarumba and surrounding areas.

🧠

Mental Health

Assessment, treatment plans, and referrals to psychologists. Specialised care for depression, anxiety, panic disorders and more.

❤️

Chronic Disease

Team-based management of diabetes, hypertension, heart disease, asthma and COPD with care plans and regular follow-up.

🔬

Procedures

Punch biopsies, skin cancer excision, Implanon insertion/removal, iron infusion, venesection, lipoma excisions, pap smears.

👨‍👩‍👧

Men & Women's Health

Healthy heart checks, cervical screening, contraception, postnatal care, menopause support and general health assessments.

💉

Vaccination

Childhood immunisations, seasonal flu, travel vaccinations and pre-employment immunity checks.

🛡️

Preventive Care

Cancer screening, osteoporosis screening, smoking and alcohol cessation programs to keep you healthy for longer.

📋

Health Checks

Four-year-old checks, 45–49 health assessments, 75+ health assessments, diabetic risk, and Aboriginal & Torres Strait Islander health checks.

🚗

Other Services

Workers compensation, employment medicals, commercial driver licence assessment, ECG, INR testing and age driver re-tests.

🌿

Holistic Approach

We treat the whole person — your physical, mental and emotional wellbeing — with care that goes beyond simply addressing symptoms.

At TMP your recovery is our priority. We always strive to give excellent and adequate attention, care and services.
