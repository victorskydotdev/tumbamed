About Tumbarumba Medical Practice

Tumbarumba Medical practice is run by qualified general practitioners who are passionate about the local community. We offer bulk billing to all patients who hold a concession card for medical consultation. Our doctors are courteous, respectful, compassionate and honest. We strive to improve patient engagement and health care outcomes. We put genuine efforts to understand the cultural needs and contexts of different patients to obtain good health outcomes.

We are located at 69 Murray Street Tumbarumba NSW 2653 Australia. We have ample of parking bays available for patients and relatives.
